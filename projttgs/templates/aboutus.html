About Automatic T.T Generator

Welcome to Automatic T.T Generator, a timetable management system for colleges and universities to schedule their organization's timetable. Automatic T.T Generator started off as a college project, aiming to form strong relationships with schools, colleges and universities to help them generate their timetable with ease and maximum efficiency, saving the organization's valuable time.

User Accounts

Automatic T.T Generator provides User accounts which gives the user the privilege to create, manipulate or delete the organization's timetable with secure access controls.
